Final

My final project was created to demonstrate our closeness to technology. Even before the pandemic we relied on our cellphones, tablets and computer on a day to day basis. Because of the pandemic we now depend on our technology more than ever.

The images were taken through FaceTime and Messenger video. Through the video call I made sure to direct my subject and take a screenshot of the desired pose. I then took the images into photoshop to convert them into RAW files so that I can manipulate them using audacity. I then use photoshop again to add a digitalized surrounding.
